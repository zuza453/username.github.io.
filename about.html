The Overview

Many unmoderated online spaces cause the spread of alt-right and fascist ideas from their supporters, onto children and young adults. These online spaces and communities often reside on websites such as Reddit, 4chan, and Voat to gather in large numbers and freely spread their ideas among each other and any newcomers. Followers of such ideas also turn to other social media platforms such as Twitter, YouTube, or Facebook to then trend hashtags, gain followers, or spread their misinformation to a larger, more diverse, audience. These spaces are populated by far-right extremists, fascists, white supremacists, men's right's activists ( which will be disscussed and explained later on), and other labels which all produce inherently violent individuals. The purpose of this page is to educate all internet users on terms and phrases used by these extremists, define them in a clear and understandable manner, and warn those who come accross this sort of language and behavior to not engage with any of the people involved. These following words and phrases are some of the most necessary vocabulary needed to fully understand the topic at hand, and be aware of the spaces we are in while online.

Vocab

Alt-right - Political identity which holds extremley conservative and rightwing views on topics such as race, gender, sequality, religion, and others.

White supremacy - Idea that white individuals are superior to those of other races and should controll all aspects of society.

Men's rights activists - Groups of men who opposte the feminist movement by making false claims about women, men losing power, and issues men face.

Fascism - A far right, authoritarian, political philosophy. Has extremist views on race, power, status, and importance.

Reddit and 4chan - Websites popular for being occupied by users who subscribe to these extremist views and use these sites to share ideas and plans with other members.

Red Pill - A reference to the Matrix, in which character Neo is presented with two pills, a red and blue one. The red pill reveals the Matrix to him, while the blue allows him to return to normal life. This is a metaphor that suggests that subscribing to alt-right views will reveal the metaphorical 'Matrix' to the person. Can also be used as "Red Pilling", refering to converting someone else to the alt-right.

Vocab

Incel - Short for 'involuntary celibate', meaning men who are unable to find any romantic or sexual partners. They often are very aggressive towards women and other men who are able to find relationships.

Men's rights activists - Groups of men who opposte the feminist movement by making false claims about women, men losing power, and issues men face.

Normie - Word used by people who frequent the internet often to describe 'normal' people, or those who are not informed on internet culture. Often used in derogatory manner.

Chads and Stacys - Names used online to refer to generic men and women who are seen as conventionally attractive by society and have no problems with aquiring romantic or sexual relationships, unlike incels.

Black Pill - A more extremist version of the red pill, in which users are urged to take action to change society. Often leads to mass murder and attacks on others.
